## Ultranutty Pecan Bars

![](https://live.staticflickr.com/65535/53348463068_75ef632fae_n.jpg)

If pecan pie is too sweet for you, this recipe is a great alternative.

Instead of being completely filled with filling, the **pecans feel lightly coated**, which makes it less sweet and gives it a wonderful texture.

It might look like the pecans will fall off when you cut it, but **surprisingly, they hold together well.**
